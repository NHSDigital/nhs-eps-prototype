Roles with access to the National Care Records Service

NHS CONNECTING FOR HEALTH (X09)

Demographic Administrator

View permissions for role: Demographic Administrator at NHS CONNECTING FOR HEALTH (X09)

This role will allow you to

  • View patient demographic details
  • Edit patient demographic details
  • Edit death notification status
  • View Summary Care Record
  • View Child Protection Plan information
  • View Reasonable Adjustments indicator
  • View Reasonable Adjustments record content
  • View existing Female Genital Mutilation - Information Sharing (FGM-IS) indicators

This role will not allow you to

  • View and update a patient's National data opt-out preference
  • Allocate NHS Numbers
  • View and print birth notifications
  • Create birth notifications
  • Create, update, or delete Reasonable Adjustments
  • View non-sensitive nationally shared available document details
  • View details and retrieve non-sensitive nationally shared available documents
  • Record new or remove existing Female Genital Mutilation - Information Sharing (FGM-IS) indicators
  • View Home Office status
  • View, add, and edit overseas visitor management status information
  • Set or amend a patient's chargeable status
